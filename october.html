October Puzzle

"Sequence"



I love game night! Or at least, I usually do. Seemingly, someone at my last game night stole my favorite game, Sequence. That board game is my greatest, most prized possession, so I've gathered everyone who was there that night to question them. Here are their responses.

Paul: I am far too polite to steal one of your games. Besides, I was playing Mouse Trap all night.

Patrick: I would never even play Sequence! I was playing Candy Land, the prime example of a well-designed board game.

Linda: I was never by the Sequence table. That game is just about getting lucky, anyways. I was playing Catan.

Pete: You call Sequence "perfect?" A real example of a perfect game is Mastermind. That's what I was playing.

Fred: I'm sorry to hear that! Fortunately, no one stole Trivial Pursuit. I love that game.

Harry: I'm happy to say that I had no involvement. Me and some folks were just playing Clue that night.

Porter: There's no practical reason I would ever steal one of your games. I'm happy just playing Kerplunk with some buddies.

Can you tell me who stole Sequence?

─────────────────

The solution to this puzzle is the name of a game night attendee.
